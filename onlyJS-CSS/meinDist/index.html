WHAT IS TERRAGENIE?

TerraGenie is a cloud-based, collaborative professional surveying and mapping platform

TerraGenie is an engineering/surveying software platform that connects with professional surveying tools such as total stations, 3D scanners and GNSS devices to an information system. TerraGenie is what GIS (Geographic information systems) was meant to be.
