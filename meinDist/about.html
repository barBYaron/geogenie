Who we are?

TerraGenie offers a cloud-based (browser based), collaborative professional surveying and mapping platform

TerraGenie connects with professional surveying equipment, such as GNSS, total stations, and scanners. The platform integrates all the data into a cloud-based central database.

TerraGenie is a professional surveying platform and an information system that works in the field or in the office with one adaptive UI/UX.

TerraGenie enables non-professionals to perform precise geodetic mapping, and offers higher efficiency to professionals.

TerraGenie is a combination between engineering/surveying platform that connects with professional surveying tools like total stations, scanners and GNSS, to an information system. In a nutshell it’s what GIS (Geographic information systems) was meant to be.

TerraGenie’s unique customization abilities do not require programming knowledge, enabling interactive and agile customization. Thanks to options to customize TerraGenie, every user can have a collection and complementary tailored-made information system.

TerraGenie enables the integration of a tailor-made applications that have spatial aspects like: civil engineering, surveying, cadastre, water management, electricity/energy management, road pavement, archaeology, accident investigation for police, and many more.
